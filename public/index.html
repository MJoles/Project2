Steps to the Game!

1 - Quarterfinals

1. Choose four of your favorite superhero movie from eight initial items.

2 - Semifinals

2. These four selections will move to the next round in which you can only pick two.

3 - Finals

3. These two options will move to the final round in which you pick the winner!

4 - Winner!

4. You will then be able to click to go back to the landing page!
